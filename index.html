The steps to making a basic grilled cheese Sandwich

  1. 1. Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. 2. Placebreadin frying pan and fry
  3. 3. Add cheese on top of bread
  4. 4. Cover with second slice of bread
  5. Turn over and fry for 2 minutes
